Make Better Use of Your Online Photo Albums

Embed interactive photo games directly into your blog
or webpage for all your vistors to enjoy.  Link from
various online resources as sources for your difference
game with the option of adding sound* bites as well!

You can either hot link to your online photos (if the image
provider allows) or package the whole game as a zip file
which can be emailed to friends or even played offline.

Great for those who worry about data charges!

*Audio support defaults to wav format

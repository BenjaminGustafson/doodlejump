The Large Hadron Collider - THE GAME

You play the part of an LHC Scientist who has stumbled on new types of matter when tinkering with

the machines detectors late one night. These new types of matter are in fact anti-matter from what

appears to be another dimension, which was opened briefly by a small black hole caused by your work!

 

Need not worry! you just need to mix all the correct (anti) matter together and discover new elements.

When all the elements have been created (32 in total) thus stablised, a new discovery will be possible!

 

Mix the matter together by touch or mouse click and draging to another element in the grey work area.

If you get two elements that mix a new element will be revealed - that can also can be mixed again!

If the work area gets full of elements then drag unused elements into the bin where they will return.
